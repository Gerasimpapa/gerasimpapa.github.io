Z Piece Mirror Test

Every new piece starts as Z, and you can use mirror to turn the current piece into S.

Controls

  • Space - Mirror current Z or S piece
  • Left / Right - Move piece
  • Down - Hard drop
  • Up - Rotate
  • Mobile single tap - Rotate
  • Mobile double tap - Mirror
  • Swipe Left / Right - Move piece
  • Swipe Down - Hard drop
